DOE Buildings
Energy Expenses

This website represents a breakdown of the energy consumption for the main educational buildings in New York City.

The infographic below dives into these numbers across 4 categories:

Electricity demand (KW)

the instant electrical power needs in kilowatts, reflects current demand

Gas (Therms)

the amount of natural gas used representing the heat content of the gas consumed

Electricity usage (KWh)

the total electrical energy consumed over time in kilowatt-hours

Total Usage (mmBTUs)

the total energy usage considering both electricity and gas usage combined
